About Me

As a designer, I not only draw inspiration from the world around me, but also the realms of music, video games, and sci-fi. From the moment I got my first Gameboy, I fell in love with the world of gaming. I developed a lifelong fascination of how technology and engaging user interfaces can immerse people in experiences with beautiful graphics and the stories crafted by game developers. Some of my favorite game franchises include The Legend of Zelda, Uncharted, and The Last of Us. I picked up the guitar in middle school, not fully realizing how important it would be in my life. After I got over the initial growing pains of learning proper form, I became obsessed with teaching myself all my favorite songs - eventually to the point where I can now play songs by ear. I’ve honed this skill for 10+ years and continue to self-educate to get better. Star Wars has been one of my science fiction franchises from the moment I watch the original trilogy as a kid. As a story that continues to be enjoyed and experienced by many generations, I am continuously inspired by the creative teams that work hard to build this world. I am actively enjoying The Mandalorian series on Disney+.
